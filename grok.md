**go.1ink.us** is the central launchpad and hub for all your 1ink.us web apps and experiments. It serves as the main portal and navigation site.

- **Role**: The “front door” to your entire creative ecosystem.
- **Importance**: High — this is often the first thing people see.

## Technology Stack
- JavaScript / TypeScript + Vite
- HTML + CSS (likely Tailwind or custom)
- Possibly some WebGL/WebGPU for visual flair

## Grok Guidelines
- **Clarity & Polish**: The site should feel clean, fast, and welcoming while showcasing your work beautifully.
- **Discoverability**: Make it easy for visitors to find and explore different projects.

- **Performance**: Fast loading is critical for a portal site.
- **Future Growth**: Design it to easily add new projects without breaking the layout.
